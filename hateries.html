HIKKADUWA TURTLE HATCHERY

About Hatchery

The Hikkaduwa Turtle Rescue Center, also known as the Hikkaduwa Sea Turtle Hatchery, is a popular tourist attraction and conservation facility located in Hikkaduwa, Sri Lanka. The primary focus of the Hikkaduwa Turtle Rescue Center is the conservation and protection of sea turtles, which are an endangered species. The center works to protect turtle eggs and nests, rehabilitate injured turtles, and release them back into the wild whenever possible. They also conduct research and educate visitors about sea turtle conservation. Visitors to the center have the opportunity to learn about various turtle species found in Sri Lanka, observe turtle hatchlings, and sometimes participate in releasing baby turtles into the ocean. The center typically operates with the help of donations and entrance fees paid by visitors, which go towards supporting its conservation efforts.

How To Get To Turtle Beach

Turtle Beach is located in Hikkaduwa, a seaside resort town in southwest of Sri Lanka. Approximately a 2 hour drive from the capital of Sri Lanka – Colombo. The Hikkaduwa turtles can usually be found swimming around directly in front of the Hikka Tranz by Cinnamon Hotel. From the main part of Hikkaduwa Beach, simply head left (when facing the ocean) until you hit a rocky divide. There are steps leading over the divide, and down to a small piece of beach in front of the HikkaTranz by Cinnamon Hotel, where the turtles like to hang.

Things we can do

Witness Nesting Turtles

If you visit during the nesting season (usually between November and April), you may have the opportunity to witness nesting turtles coming ashore to lay their eggs. Hatchery staff can guide you on proper conduct and help you understand the nesting process.

Learn about Turtles

Gain knowledge about the different turtle species found in Sri Lanka, including their habitats, behavior, and conservation status. The staff at the hatchery will provide valuable information and educational sessions.

Observe Turtle Hatchlings

See adorable baby turtles hatching from their eggs and making their way to the ocean. This awe-inspiring experience allows you to witness the crucial moment when the hatchlings start their journey.
